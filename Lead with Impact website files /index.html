Lovin' Life Ministries has church locations across the country, each unique in size and style, but all with one goal. That goal is to share the breaking news and to invite others to do the same. There is something exciting about helping create a national church that impacts local cities, links together cultures, brings life to individuals and families, and builds a generation unlike any other; a generation of peace.

Lovin' Life provides both careers, internships, and opportunities to give back as a volunteer at your local church and at the National office in New York City so that you can build your church home and your professional career. Many positions require completion of the 8-week Lovin' Life Leadership Program, which you can learn about here.

If you are interested in joining your local LLM team or finding job opportunities with the national team in NYC, click below to see available job openings, internships, and volunteer positions.
